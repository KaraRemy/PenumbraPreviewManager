# Quick Start Guide - Penumbra Preview Manager (PPM)

Welcome to **Penumbra Preview Manager**! This quick guide will walk you through setting up your display preferences, adding your first main mod preview, configuring option thumbnails, and using shortcuts for a fast workflow.

---

## Step 1: Initial Setup & Settings

Before setting up previews, take a quick look at the plugin settings to customize display options to your liking.

1. Open the settings window in chat using:
   ```text
   /ppm settings
   ```
2. **Adjust Crop Settings**: Change the crop methods to match your preference.
   - *Recommendation*: Set **Main Preview Import Crop Method** and **Mod-Option Preview Crop Method** to **No Crop (Preserve Aspect)**.
3. For explanations of all other display, button, and integration options, check the [FAQ.md](./FAQ.md).

---

## Step 2: Setting Your First Main Mod Preview

Copying a thumbnail directly from **XIVModArchive (XMA)** to your system clipboard is by far the fastest and easiest method.

1. Select a mod inside **Penumbra**.
2. Click the **"Search XMA"** button on the preview bar inside Penumbra. Your default web browser will open XMA's search page with the mod name and author already filled out.
   - *Note*: If XMA returns no results, try deleting extra version numbers or tags from the search box.
3. On the XMA mod page, **right-click** the preview image you want and select **Copy Image**.
4. Return to FINAL FANTASY XIV and click the **"Paste Clipboard"** button inside Penumbra.

---

## Step 3: Adding Previews to Individual Mod Options

You can assign individual thumbnail previews to specific option dropdowns or checkboxes (e.g. gear variants, textures, or toggles).

1. Open the main manager window in chat using:
   ```text
   /ppm
   ```
2. Select your mod, then navigate the **Mod Option Previews** dropdowns to select the specific option group and option name you want to customize (e.g., `Top` -> `Shirt Only`).
3. **Capture an Image**: Use the built-in Windows Snipping tool shortcut:
   ```text
   Win + Shift + S
   ```
   Drag a quick crop over your in-game character or gear preview. This automatically saves the cropped image to your clipboard.
4. Click **"Paste Clipboard"** under the option preview panel in PPM to assign it.

---

## Pro-Tip: Full-Scale Image Zooming

At any time, hold down the **Middle Mouse Button** (mouse scroll wheel click) over any main mod preview card or option trigger icon to overlay a full-scale zoomed preview on your screen.
